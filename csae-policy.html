Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Policy

Paish Qadam App

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

Paish Qadam strictly prohibits any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Any content, behaviour, or communication that promotes, depicts, or facilitates CSAE is not allowed on our platform.

2. Admin-Moderated Environment

Our app is fully admin-moderated. All content uploaded, shared, or discussed is reviewed either before posting or through regular checks to ensure compliance with our policies. Any suspicious or harmful content is immediately flagged and removed.

3. Reporting and Response

Users can report any inappropriate or harmful content through the in-app reporting feature. All reports are reviewed within 24 hours. Confirmed violations result in immediate content removal and account suspension or banning.

4. Cooperation with Authorities

We fully cooperate with law enforcement and relevant authorities. If CSAE content is detected or reported, we preserve necessary data and forward all information to the appropriate agencies for investigation.

5. Legal Compliance

This app adheres to the Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act (PECA) 2016 in Pakistan and applicable international standards. We are committed to creating a safe, secure, and abuse-free environment for all users.
